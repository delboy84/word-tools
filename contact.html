Bug Reports

Found a tool that's not working right? Let us know the tool name, what you entered, and what went wrong. Screenshots help too.

Feature Requests

Got an idea for a new calculator, solver, or utility? We're always building new tools and would love to hear what you need.

Calculation Questions

If a calculator result doesn't match your expectations, we're happy to explain the methodology and check if something needs fixing.

Tells us what you love, what you'd improve, or what's missing. We read every message.

We're a small team and we do this in our spare time, so please allow a few days for a response. We read every email and we'll get back to you as soon as we can.
