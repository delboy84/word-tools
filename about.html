What is Thinker Toolbox?

Thinker Toolbox is a collection of free, browser-based utilities that actually work. No sign-ups, no subscriptions, no data harvesting. Just tools that do what they say on the tin.

We got fed up with word puzzle solvers that are buried under clutter, colour pickers that want your email, and tip calculators that track your dinner bills. So we built our own. Then we put them all in one place, made them work offline, and called it Thinker Toolbox.

The name used to be a bit misleading — but we reckon Thinker Toolbox covers it. We've got word puzzles, financial calculators, a colour picker, a Sudoku solver, a tip calculator, and a cooking converter. Tools for thinkers, all in one toolbox.

The Tools

🔤

Crossword Solver

Stuck on a crossword? Type in your pattern with ? for unknown letters and we'll find all matching words.

🔄

Anagram Solver

Got a jumble of letters? Find every word that can be made from them, sorted by length and Scrabble score.

🟩

Wordle Solver

Enter your guesses and the green/yellow/grey feedback. We'll narrow down the possibilities and suggest your best next guess.

🀄

Scrabble Word Finder

Enter your rack letters and any board letters. We'll find every valid word, sorted by Scrabble score. Bingo words get a ⭐.

🏠

Mortgage Calculator

Calculate monthly payments, view amortization schedules, see how overpayments save you money, compare mortgage deals, check stamp duty, and find out how much you could borrow.

📊

Percentage Calculator

Work out percentages of numbers, percentage increases and decreases, reverse percentages (remove VAT), and more. Live calculation as you type.

📈

Savings Calculator

See how your savings grow with compound interest. Plan regular contributions, set savings goals, and view growth charts showing contributions vs interest earned.

🎨

Colour Picker

Pick a colour and instantly see complementary, triadic, analogous, and other harmonious palettes. Includes WCAG contrast checking and — snap a photo to extract dominant colours.

🔢

Sudoku Solver

Stuck on a Sudoku? Enter the puzzle and get the solution instantly. Or use hint mode to reveal just one cell at a time.

💰

Tip Calculator

Quick tip calculation with presets (15%, 18%, 20%, 25%) and bill splitting between friends. Round up or split exactly.

🍳

Cooking Converter

Convert cups to grams (with ingredient-specific densities — a cup of flour ≠ a cup of sugar), scale recipes, and look up common conversions.

Why these ones?

Works offline. Once you've visited, the tools cache themselves in your browser. No internet? No problem. On the train, in a café, in a dead zone — they still work.

Works on your phone. Every tool is designed mobile-first. Big buttons, readable text, no pinching and zooming.

Fast. No server round-trips for calculations. Everything runs in your browser. The word puzzles load a 168,000-word dictionary instantly.

Private. No tracking, no analytics, no cookies banner. What you type stays in your browser. We don't even have a server to send it to.

No accounts. No sign-up, no email, no password. Just open and use.

About the Word List

The dictionary used in the word puzzle tools is the ENABLE word list (Public Domain), containing over 168,000 Scrabble-valid words. It also includes the official Wordle answer list for the Wordle Solver.

It's great for casual use, but if you're training for competitive Scrabble, you should note that a small number of words added in later Scrabble dictionary updates (such as QI and ZA) are not included in the ENABLE list.
