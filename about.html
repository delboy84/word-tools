What is Thinker Toolbox?

Thinker Toolbox is a collection of 19 free, browser-based utilities that actually work. No sign-ups, no subscriptions, no data harvesting. Just tools that do what they say on the tin.

We got fed up with word puzzle solvers that are buried under clutter, colour pickers that want your email, and tip calculators that track your dinner bills. So we built our own. Then we put them all in one place, made them work offline, and called it Thinker Toolbox.

The name used to be a bit misleading — but Thinker Toolbox covers it. Finance calculators, solar & energy tools, date calculators, word puzzles, and everyday utilities. Tools for thinkers, all in one toolbox.

Why these ones?

Works offline. Once you've visited, the tools cache themselves in your browser. No internet? No problem. On the train, in a café, in a dead zone — they still work.

Works on your phone. Every tool is designed mobile-first. Big buttons, readable text, no pinching and zooming.

Fast. No server round-trips for calculations. Everything runs in your browser. The word puzzles load a 168,000-word dictionary instantly. Finance calculators export to PDF on demand.

Private. We use Google AdSense which may set cookies for personalised ads. You can accept or reject these in the cookie banner. Your calculator inputs stay in your browser and are never sent anywhere. PDF reports are generated locally — nothing leaves your device.

No accounts. No sign-up, no email, no password. Just open and use.

About the Word List

The dictionary used in the word puzzle tools is the ENABLE word list (Public Domain), containing over 168,000 Scrabble-valid words. It also includes the official Wordle answer list for the Wordle Solver.

It's great for casual use, but if you're training for competitive Scrabble, you should note that a small number of words added in later Scrabble dictionary updates (such as QI and ZA) are not included in the ENABLE list.
