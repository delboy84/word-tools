Frequently Asked Questions

How many letters can I enter?

Up to 12 letters. The solver finds all valid words from 2 letters up to the full set. Results are grouped by length for easy browsing.

Can I specify that a word must contain certain letters?

Yes — use the "contains" filter to require specific letters in the results. For example, enter "SATIRE" and filter for words containing "Q" to find words that use your letters plus a Q you might place on the board.

Are the Scrabble scores accurate?

The scores shown are the base Scrabble values for each word. They don't account for letter or word multipliers on the board, as those depend on placement. The scores help you quickly identify the highest-value words from your rack.

Does it work offline?

Yes — the entire dictionary is cached in your browser after your first visit. Works offline, no internet required.
