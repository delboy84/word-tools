Frequently Asked Questions

Does it count the start and end dates?

By default, the days-between calculation excludes the start date but includes the end date (like counting days remaining). You can toggle this to include or exclude either date depending on what you need.

How many UK bank holidays are there?

England and Wales typically have 8 bank holidays per year. Scotland has 9 (different dates). Northern Ireland has 10. The government occasionally adds extra ones. the calculator includes all confirmed dates including any additional holidays.

Can it count working days only?

Yes. you can exclude weekends and/or bank holidays from the count. This is useful for contract deadlines, project timelines, and notice periods that specify "working days" rather than calendar days.

Does it account for leap years?

Yes. The calculator correctly handles leap years (including the rule that century years are only leap years if divisible by 400). Date calculations are always exact.

Is my data private?

Yes. All calculations run in your browser.
