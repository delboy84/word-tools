Frequently Asked Questions

What's the standard tip in the UK?

10% is common for good service in restaurants. Some restaurants add a service charge (usually 12.5%). check your bill. Tipping in pubs isn't expected, though you can offer to buy the bar staff a drink for good service.

Should I tip on the pre-tax or post-tax amount?

In the UK, VAT is included in the displayed price, so you tip on the bill total. In the US, tipping on the pre-tax amount is standard, though some people tip on the total including tax.

Does this handle rounding?

Yes. you can round each person's share up or down to avoid fiddly amounts. The calculator shows both the exact split and a rounded version.

Is my data private?

Yes. All calculations run in your browser.
