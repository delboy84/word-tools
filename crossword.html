How to Use This Crossword Solver

  1. Type your pattern — Enter the letters you know and use ? for unknown letters. For example, C??SS?O?D finds CROSSWORD.
  2. See matching words — Results appear instantly as you type, showing all words that match your pattern.
  3. Filter by length — Narrow results to a specific word length if needed.

Use Cases

Stuck on a crossword clue. You've got a few letters and just can't figure out the rest. Enter what you know and the solver shows all possible words that fit. It's like having a dictionary that only shows entries matching your pattern.

Codeword puzzles. Codewords give you a few starting letters and you need to fill in the rest. This solver is perfect for finding words that fit a specific letter pattern.

Word game verification. Not sure if a word exists? Type it in with all known letters and see if it appears in results. The dictionary contains over 168,000 words.

Frequently Asked Questions

How many words does the dictionary contain?

Over 168,000 words from the ENABLE word list, which is the standard reference for word games. It includes common English words, inflections, and many less common terms.

Can I search for words of a specific length?

Yes — the pattern length automatically determines word length. A pattern like ??? shows all 3-letter words. You can also combine known and unknown letters.

Does it work offline?

Yes — once you've visited the page, the entire dictionary is cached in your browser. The solver works completely offline, which is handy for doing crosswords on the commute.

Is it valid for competitive Scrabble?

The word list is based on ENABLE, which is widely accepted for casual play and online word games. For official competitive Scrabble, you should use the CSW (Collins Scrabble Words) or TWL (Tournament Word List) dictionaries. Some words added in recent CSW updates may not be in our list.
